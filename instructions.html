Instructions - Use Case

Our goal is to test this system in a real educational environments and measure how the system can help participants to collaborate to create open-based resources. The use case relates to an ongoing class, such as a lecture, that is a live event. A number of students or participants can express their thoughts as the lecture goes on. The collaboration space is kept in a wiki page and the Expression Widgets JetPack application represents the user's input into that space. If you have suggestions or if you are willing to test this at your location please keep in contact - mgalli at mgalli dot com.
